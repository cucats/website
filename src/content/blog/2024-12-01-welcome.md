Welcome to the CUCaTS blog! This is where we'll share news, tutorials, and updates about our events and activities.

## About CUCaTS

The Cambridge University Computing and Technology Society (CUCaTS) is a student-run society dedicated to promoting computing and technology among students at the University of Cambridge.

## What We Do

We organise a variety of events throughout the year, including:

- **Workshops** - Hands-on sessions covering topics from web development to machine learning
- **Talks** - Guest speakers from industry and academia
- **Hackathons** - Collaborative coding events
- **Social events** - Opportunities to meet fellow tech enthusiasts

## Get Involved

Stay tuned to this blog for updates on upcoming events, tutorials, and more!
